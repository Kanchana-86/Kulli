About the Project

In a world where nature’s wonders are fading away, Species in Pieces invites you to rediscover and protect biodiversity. Discover the lives of 30 endangered species through digital art.

How to Experience the Journey

  1. Explore the website and find 30 shapes, each for an endangered species.
  2. Click a shape to see the animal’s story with animations.
  3. Learn about conservation and actions to protect species.

Watch the Story

What Makes This Project Special?

  • Pure CSS Art: Polygonal art designed with CSS3.
  • Immersive Learning: Combines visuals and storytelling.
  • A Call to Action: Inspires action for biodiversity.
