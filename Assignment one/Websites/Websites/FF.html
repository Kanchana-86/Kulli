What is "Fishing the Feed"?

Fishing the Feed is an interactive website that exposes the environmental and social impacts of the aquaculture industry's reliance on wild-caught fish for feed. It reveals how billions of wild fish are harvested to produce fishmeal and fish oil for farmed seafood, leading to overfishing, ecosystem disruption, and food insecurity in vulnerable communities. The website aims to raise awareness and encourage action towards more sustainable aquaculture practices.

Who Created the Website?

The website was created by the Changing Markets Foundation, an organization dedicated to promoting sustainable market practices. They partnered with Fiasco Design, a creative agency known for crafting engaging digital experiences, to design and build the site.
